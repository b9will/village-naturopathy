About Dr. Heather

I grew up a naturally anxious and easily stressed-out person. I get it.

I became a naturopath because I saw too many women dismissed by a system that treats symptoms in isolation. My undergraduate degree in Psychology from the University of Ottawa fostered my passion for addressing mental health, and my postgraduate studies highlighted how inseparable mental health is from physical health.

Village Naturopathy was founded as a virtual clinic to bring quality, specific care to busy, driven, stressed-out women — without the limitation of geography.

The Approach

Root-cause care, not symptom management.

Every protocol starts with your story. I look at the whole picture — sleep, stress, digestion, hormones, environment — because symptoms are signals, not the problem itself.

Using clinical nutrition, evidence-based herbal medicine, holistic counselling, CBT, and functional testing where needed, we build a plan that fits your real life. As a Certified HeartMath Practitioner, I integrate HeartMath techniques into my Holistic Stress Solution approach to help you build measurable resilience.

Whether you’re navigating anxiety and overwhelm, painful or irregular cycles, PCOS, endometriosis, digestive concerns, skin conditions, or simply feeling burnt out — we get to the root cause and create lasting change.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is naturopathic medicine?

Naturopathic medicine is a distinct system of primary care that uses natural therapeutics to identify and treat the root cause of illness. It combines modern science with traditional healing methods including clinical nutrition, herbal medicine, and lifestyle counselling.

What can I expect from a virtual consultation?

Your initial consultation is 75 minutes and includes a complete health assessment and personalised treatment plan, with possible lab requisitions. All consultations are delivered via secure video call. A complimentary 15-minute discovery call is available first to make sure we’re the right fit.

What conditions does Village Naturopathy treat?

Village Naturopathy specialises in stress and nervous system support, hormone balancing (PCOS, endometriosis, irregular cycles, menopause), gut health (IBS, SIBO, food sensitivities), mental health (anxiety, depression), skin conditions (acne, eczema), and energy and longevity.

How do virtual consultations work?

All consultations are delivered via secure video call. You’ll receive a link before your appointment — all you need is a quiet space and a reliable internet connection. Virtual care allows us to serve patients across Ontario and the United Kingdom.

What diagnostic testing do you offer?

Village Naturopathy offers DUTCH hormone testing, GI-MAP digestive analysis, organic acid testing (OAT), blood work, mould and mycotoxin panels, and hair mineral analysis. Testing is recommended based on individual needs.

Do you see patients outside Ontario?

Yes. Dr. Robinson is a full member of the Association of Naturopathic Practitioners (UK) and sees patients in both Ontario and the United Kingdom via virtual consultation.
