Join our practice

We’re building something different.

Village Naturopathy is a virtual-first practice serving women across Ontario and the United Kingdom. We believe in collaborative, evidence-informed care — and we’re always looking for like-minded practitioners to grow with us.

Who we’re looking for

Whether you’re an established naturopathic doctor, a newly registered ND, or a complementary practitioner in a related field, we’d love to hear from you. We’re especially interested in practitioners with experience or interest in:

  • Women’s hormonal health & fertility
  • Stress, anxiety & nervous system regulation
  • Gut health & functional digestive care
  • Mental health & psychotherapy
  • Functional lab testing & interpretation
  • Botanical medicine & clinical nutrition

What we offer

We provide the infrastructure so you can focus on patient care:

  • A fully virtual practice — work from anywhere in Ontario or the UK
  • Booking, scheduling, and patient management systems already in place
  • Marketing, branding, and patient acquisition support
  • A collaborative environment with shared clinical resources
  • Flexible arrangements — full-time, part-time, or associate models

Our values

We’re root-cause practitioners, not symptom managers. Every member of our team is committed to thorough assessment, advanced testing when appropriate, personalised treatment plans, and genuine patient relationships. If that resonates with you, we should talk.

Get in touch

There are no formal postings right now — we prefer conversations over applications. If you’re interested in joining Village Naturopathy or exploring a collaboration, reach out directly.
