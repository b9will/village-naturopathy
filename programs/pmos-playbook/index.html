PMOS refers to the pattern of premenstrual overwhelm that goes beyond typical PMS — involving nervous system dysregulation, hormonal cascades, and cyclical mood and energy crashes that disrupt daily life. It’s not a formal diagnosis, but a framework for understanding the full-body impact of your cycle.
