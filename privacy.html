Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect certain information automatically through analytics tools, including pages viewed, time spent on each page, referring website, browser type, and general geographic region. If you submit a form (such as our booking request), we collect the information you provide — your name, email address, and any details about your health concerns.

Google Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our site. GA4 collects anonymised usage data such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources. It does not collect personally identifiable information. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Meta Pixel

We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to deliver relevant content. The pixel collects data about your browsing activity on our site, which may be used to show you targeted ads on Meta platforms. No health-related data is shared with Meta.

You can manage your ad preferences through Facebook’s Ad Settings.

Cloudflare

Our website is hosted and served through Cloudflare, which provides content delivery, security, and basic analytics. Cloudflare may collect technical data such as your IP address and browser information to protect against malicious traffic. This data is processed in accordance with Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to support analytics and advertising tools described above. These cookies help us understand site usage and measure campaign performance. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our site links to third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Jane App — used for appointment booking. Information you enter through Jane App is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Fullscript — used for supplement dispensing. Purchases made through Fullscript are subject to their privacy policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the analytics and advertising platforms described above, and only to the extent necessary to operate and improve our website.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the default settings of each platform (typically 14 months for GA4). Form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and facilitate care.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email drheather@villagenaturopathy.com.
