Chronic stress isn’t just mental — it disrupts sleep, digestion, hormones, immunity, and energy. We use evidence-based naturopathic strategies to calm the nervous system, rebuild resilience, and address the physical fallout of long-term stress.

  • Generalised anxiety disorder
  • Panic attacks
  • Burnout & adrenal fatigue
  • Insomnia & sleep disturbances
  • Nervous system dysregulation
